MARCHROMT30a Edit 2b 96 EP

MARCHROMT30a Edit 2b 96 first appeared as the Japanese bonus track for recent album ‘Syro’ becoming an instant favourite of those that heard it. The song now comes to vinyl for a global audience, backed with a new version of Syro track ‘XMAS_EVET10 [120] (thanaton3 mix)’ and an alternate take of MARCHROMT. It's released on white label 12" with hand stamp.
